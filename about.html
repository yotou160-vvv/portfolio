About Me

Hi there! I’m Violet Zhang, an artist who loves exploring ideas through visual arts, with a Bachelor of Arts in Animation and a background in fine arts. I’ve been fortunate to create award-winning independent short films and contribute to professional projects in the film industry.

I love how visuals capture emotion and tell stories that linger. Outside of working, I spend my time sketching, experimenting with colors, or seeking inspiration around me.

Feel free to reach out—I’d love to connect, chat about art, or collaborate on something exciting!
