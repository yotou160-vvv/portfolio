UNDER THE BLACK HOLE

Role: Directing, Screenplay Writing, Storyboarding Color Script and Background Design Animation, Poster Design

Film Introduction:

A mysterious black hole appears over the city, and people believe cleaning up a circular object can make it disappear. The main character, a staff member who cleans the circle, trembles inside because he hides the object — a Walkman.

This graduate project was completed with my classmates in four months during the COVID-19 pandemic in China. I defined the art style during pre-production and contributed extensively in production, including color scripting, background design, and creating the majority of key poses for the most intense action shots.

GO!GET IT

Role: Directing, Screenplay Writing, Storyboarding Color Script and Background Design Animation, Poster Design

Film Introduction:

This work tells the story of the friendship between the protagonist and her three-legged dog. They were inseparable during childhood, but as the protagonist grew up, they gradually drifted apart. When she finally reflects on their bond, she faces the reality of her dog aging and having only a limited time left.

This animated short was completed during COVID with two friends at the Communication University of China. Due to lockdown restrictions, all communication and production were carried out entirely online.
